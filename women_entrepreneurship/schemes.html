Government Schemes

Key schemes available for women entrepreneurs in India

💰
Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana
🏛️ Ministry of Finance
Provides collateral-free loans to women who want to start or grow a small business. No need to pledge any property or assets to get this loan. Women entrepreneurs get priority and a lower interest rate compared to others.
Loans from ₹50,000 to ₹10 Lakh No collateral required Available at all major banks
🏭
Stand-Up India Scheme
🏛️ Ministry of Finance
Designed to help women entrepreneurs set up manufacturing, service or trading businesses. The scheme provides larger loans than Mudra and is suitable for women who want to build a proper business unit with employees.
Loans from ₹10 Lakh to ₹1 Crore Repayment period up to 7 years For manufacturing & services
🌸
Mahila Udyam Nidhi Scheme
🏛️ Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI)
Supports women who want to start small-scale industries by providing financial assistance at low interest rates. The scheme also helps existing women-owned businesses expand their operations and hire more employees.
Assistance up to ₹10 Lakh Low interest rates Available via SIDBI branches
🎓
STEP – Support to Training & Employment
🏛️ Ministry of Women & Child Development
A free skill training program for women across India covering areas like agriculture, handicrafts, tailoring, computer skills and more. After completing the training, women receive a government certificate that helps them start their own business or find employment.
Completely free training Government certificate provided Priority for rural women
